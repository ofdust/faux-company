About

Tea-Time Tours is a London-based tour company specializing in one of a kind tours of the city of London itself. Come with us on a guided tour, led by London locals, as we take you across the heart of London to some of the best must-see sites in the city in one of our state of the art tour busses. Participants will get to see the best that London has to offer with commentary and information from our experienced tour guides, travelling in the comfort of one of our state of the art tour busses. Entry tickets for all ticketed stops is included in the price of your ticket, meaning you have nothing to worry about but stepping onto the bus.
